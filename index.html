🌱 Composting FAQs 🌱

Composty is an interactive game (above) that provides a quick idea of what you can and can't compost. It simplifies composting for young children! 🌎

Composting is a system where you can dispose of organic matter, like fruits, veggie scraps, hair, peels, & more! Composting works because organic matter can break down naturally, then eventually turns into soil. Worms can hasten up the composting process by acting as super-decomposers. 🍎

Composting is an easy way to have a positive impact on the environment! It enriches soil and reduces the need or chemical fertilizers, which are harmful to the environment. Almost 20x of methane emissions in the US come from food being unable to breakdown in a landfill. This makes composting one of the best things you can do for the enviornment. Furthermore, it can help reduce methane emissions and lower your carbon footprint. 🤩

Composting can be done entirely from home! Watch this cool video by peppa pig to learn how to compost at home. 🏠
